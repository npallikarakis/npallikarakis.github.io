About

I bridge theoretical mathematics and industrial applications, combining rigorous academic research with practical engineering expertise. As a PhD in Applied Mathematics and Senior Data Scientist at n&k Technology, I solve complex inverse problems using deep learning and numerical methods for both scientific discovery and semiconductor manufacturing optimization.

My research spans inverse electromagnetic scattering, machine learning architectures, and numerical methods for PDEs. With 13+ years of combined academic and industry experience—including 7+ years at n&k Technology and 4+ years in postdoctoral research and teaching—I bring unique expertise at the intersection of theory and application. My work addresses real-world semiconductor metrology challenges while advancing fundamental mathematical theory.
